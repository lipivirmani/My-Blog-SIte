About Me

This is what I do.

Hey, I’m Lipi — an engineer by degree, a creator by passion, and a bit of a quiet rebel at heart. This space is a reflection of the many parts of me — the girl who codes, dreams big, questions norms, and is learning to take up space unapologetically.

I graduated in Computer Science from MAIT, and somewhere along the way, I realized that life isn’t just about clean code and perfect resumes. It’s also about messy growth, uncomfortable changes, spontaneous ideas, and showing up for yourself every day — even when it’s hard.

I’m currently exploring the world of machine learning, web development, and digital experiences. But more than that, I’m learning how to express myself — whether it’s through design, blogs, or just having honest conversations.
Public speaking used to terrify me — now I’m walking straight into it. Meeting new people used to feel out of my comfort zone — now I chase it. Because deep down, I know I’m meant for something bigger, and I’m done waiting for permission to grow into it.
This blog isn’t just a portfolio or a project — it’s a journal of becoming.
Of trying. Of failing. Of laughing at my own code. Of finding beauty in late-night ideas and turning them into something real. Of breaking out of the mold, especially when it feels safer to stay inside it.

So whether you’re here because you’re into tech, creativity, self-growth, or just curious — I’m glad you made it.
Let’s keep figuring it out. Together.
